The Fitness Hub

Gym Equipment/Facilities!

Free Parking!

Want free parking near the gym?
don't worry we thought of you! The Fitness Hub has a free car park just outside the door!

Pin Entry System!

Due to our gym being 24hours we have got a pin entry system,
as staff will not be present in the gym at night.
When you register for the gym you will be given a pin which you can use to get in and out of the gym at any hour.

Vending Machines!

Everyone has those days where you forget to bring something,
if it is to work, to the gym, etc.
Well if you do forget one of your gym products dont worry our vending machine will have it!
We have water, energy drinks, protein shakes and protein bars.
We even have you convered if you need a towel or a padlock!

Changing/locker Room!

Feel free to use the changing room and lockers which are available 24 hours.

We have loadsof lockers available at the gym to use to put your stuff in.
Please make sure you padlock the locker you are using.
You can bring your own padlock or buy one for Â£4 at the vending machine.

Shower Room!

Feel free to go and use the shower room, which is located in the changing rooms.
The shower room is open 24hours, apart from a cleaner will come and clean it between 1am and 3am.

Free Weights!

You want to work on your muscles?
Come over to the free weights section which dumbbells start from 2kg all the way to 50kg.
We even have weight benches and squat racks!

Fixed Resistance Weights!

If your not comfortable using the free weights, we have plenty of fixed Resistance weights.
These weights are perfect for starters at the gym, as you you have resistance weights for each part of your body.
They will show you what muscles you will be working for that machine, get the same weight options as free weights
and they show you what to do at each machine via a diagram.
Do not get us wrong they are not just for starters at the gym, anyone can use them! Some people prefer them to free weights.
It all comes down to you as the trainers preferences.

Fitness Studio!

Our gym offers free classes to all members. If your a beginner or an expert, we got you covered!
from Cardio to muscle toning! No one will miss out as we have classes for everyone, in our studio spaces!
