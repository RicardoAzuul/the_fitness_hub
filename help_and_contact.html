Joining the gym!

How do I join the Gym?

You want to join the gym? Don't worry we have made it easy for you.You can join online by clicking on join now and fill out the form.
Once you filled out the form you will receive an email with your pin number.
You can also apply at the gym at the kiosk.

How old do I need to be to start the gym?

The minimum age to start at the gym is 16 years old.
Age 16 to 17 years old have to do an induction day on your first start day at the gym.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

This is a no contract gym, so you can leave any time with no notice and no cancelling fee.

Equipment/ Facilities!

Will I be shown how to use the equipment?

Yes, you can book a free induction day to show you around the gym and show you how to use the equipment.
We do recommend that anyone who has not been a member of a gym before to take an induction day.

Do you have parking on site?

Yes, not only we have our own car park but it is free parking as well!
