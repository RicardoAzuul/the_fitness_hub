Membership Benefits

Freeze your account

Your not able to get to the gym for a while
and do not want to be charged the full price?
As a member you can freeze your account for just £3 a month.
One phone call and your account is frozen.
Contact our team on 01234 555897.

Get a personal trainer for a discount of 25%

When you become a member at our gym you will get a 25% discount on any personal trainer of your choice.

No sign up fee

There is no signing up fee when you join our gym. All you need to do is fill out the application form and your a member. I know how easy could we make it!

First Month Free

You ever wanted to go to the gym, but not sure if it is to your liking? At our gym we give you the first month as a free trial period!

Once a month bring a friend
for half price

Once a month you can bring a friend, who is not a member for half price! Normally a day pass is £4.99 but your friend can come in for just £2.50.

No cancellation fee

If you are a member but decide that gym is not for you anymore, you can cancel your gym membership with us for free.

Get earning points for going to the gym

We now give you two points every five times you come to the gym. Every time you get 10 points you get a 10% discount of your next pay or your family/friends next pay.
